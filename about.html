Our Mission

Develop, foster and enhance the spirit of togetherness within the Chinese community

Create the platform for our people to meet, work and play in harmony and respect

Foster the culture base that was handed to us by our parents, particularly in the areas of heritage, family loyalty and respect for our elders and leaders

Respond to the needs of our community, where possible, to the best of our ability

Share with the wider community aspects of our culture
